Patented Autonomous Mine Rover

Designing reliable systems for extreme subsurface environments.

Application no : 202541104590

The Challenge & My Solution

The Problem: In hazardous mining environments, human safety is at high risk due to the presence of toxic gases, unstable terrain, and poor visibility. Current exploration methods often rely on manual intervention, which increases the danger to personnel.

My Solution: The main concern inside the mine is the lack of visibility as it gets deeper the vision is almost zero. So the rover is integrated with LiDAR A1M8 sensor so that we can easily detect the obstacles and also helps us to detect the cracks and debris.
